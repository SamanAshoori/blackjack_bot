# Blackjack Monte Carlo

Blackjack is a card game where the goal is to get as close to 21 without going over.
Dealer will only show one face-up card

Card Values:
- A: 1 or 11
- K,Q,J : 10
- 1-9: 1-9

Actions (simplified):
- hit (take another card)
- stand (stop taking cards)

At the end of both turns - the player with highest total (below or equal to 21 wins) if either player is above 21 then you lose (go bust)
